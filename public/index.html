Compostify

Compostify is a platform that allows you to share leftover food with nearby NGOs, who can then donate it to those in need. You can also choose to share the food with agencies that handle food decomposition. The platform connects people who have excess food with organizations that can either distribute it to the needy or use it for eco-friendly purposes. NGOs and food agencies can easily collect the shared food through Compostify, making it a convenient way to reduce waste and help both the community and the environment.

This project was developed as part of the HackwithIndia hackathon which was organised by Microsoft India.

Web : https://compostify.vercel.app/

Github : https://github.com/alwaysinmy-rhythm/HackWithIndia
